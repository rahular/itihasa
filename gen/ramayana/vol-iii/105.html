Yuddha Kanda: Chapter 105

ततो युद्धपरिश्रान्तं समरे चिन्तया स्थितम्। रावणं चाग्रतो दृष्ट्वा युद्धाय समुपस्थितम्॥ दैवतैश्च समागम्य द्रष्टुमभ्यागतो रणम्। उपगम्याब्रवीद्राममगस्त्यो भगवांस्तदा॥
Then the revered Agastya. who desirous of witnessing the fight, had along with the deities come there-seeing Rävaņa spent with the toil of conflict, staying in the field plunged in thought, and stationed before Rāma for engaging in encounter-addressed Rama, drawing near to him, saying.

राम राम महाबाहो शृणु गुह्यं सनातनम्। येन सर्वानरीन्वत्स समरे विजयिष्यसे॥ आदित्यहृदयं पुण्यं सर्वशत्रुविनाशनम्। जयावहं जपं नित्यमक्षयं परमं शिवम्॥ सर्वमङ्गलमाङ्गल्यं सर्वपापप्रणाशनम्। चिन्ताशोकप्रशमनमायुर्वर्धनमुत्तमम्॥
Rāma, Rāma, O mighty-armed one, hearken to the eternal secret, whereby my child, you will conquer all foes in fight-Adityahțdya,* sacred, capable of destroying all foes bringing victory the recitation, enduring and in destructible, and supremely good; fraught with all welfare removing every sin, chasing away anxiety and grief, bringing length of days; and excellent. *Lit.-The heart of the Sun-the designation of a Vedic Hymn.

रश्मिमन्तं समुद्यन्तं देवासुरनमस्कृतम्। पूजयस्व विवस्वन्तं भास्करं भुवनेश्वरम्॥ सर्वदेवात्मको ह्येष तेजस्वी रश्मिभावनः। एष देवासुरगणाँल्लोकान्पाति गभस्तिभिः॥
Do you worship Vivasvata's offspring-the Sun-lord of the world, furnished with rays, who make people engage in work, and who is bowed down to by deities and Asuras. This effulgent one, producing rays is instinct with the spirits of all the deities; and he with his rays rule all creatures and the hosts of celestials and Asuras.

एष ब्रह्मा च विष्णुश्च शिवः स्कन्दः प्रजापतिः। महेन्द्रो धनदः कालो यमः सोमो ह्यपांपतिः॥ पितरो वसवः साध्या अश्विनौ मरुतो मनुः। वायुर्वह्निः प्रजाः प्राण ऋतुकर्मा प्रभाकरः।।।।।

नमः पूर्वाय गिरये पश्चिमायाद्रये नमः। ज्योतिर्गणानां पतये दिनाधिपतये नमः॥
Salutation to the Eastern mount and the mount of the West. Salutation to the lord of the stellar bodies and salutation also to the lord of day.

जयाय जयभद्राय हर्यश्वाय नमो नमः। नमो नमः सहस्रांशो आदित्याय नमो नमः॥
Salutation and salutation to him that bring victory, and the joy that spring up from victory; and to him of yellow steeds. Salutation, salutation, O thousand-rayed one! Salutation and salutation to Aditya.

नम उग्राय वीराय सारङ्गाय नमो नमः। नमः पद्मप्रबोधाय प्रचण्डाय नमोऽस्तु ते॥
Salutation to him that keep his senses under subjection; Salutation and salutation to the Hero, (i) and to Saranga (k) and to him that awaken the Lotus (1) (i) Him that lead the senses, and that is endowed with the prowess of slaying Tripura etc. (k) Him that deserve the pranava, the holies formula in all Hindu Scripture. (1) That awaken the external lotus as will as the lotus of the heart.

And (salutation) to you O fierce one. Salutation to the Lord himself of Brahmā, Iśána (m) and Achchyuta, (n) and to Sura (o) and to him that constitutes the knowledge of Aditya, and to him that unfold me and not, me and to the devourer of all, and to the form of the destroyer of the darkness of ignorance. तप्तचामीकराभाय हरये विश्वकर्मणे। नमस्तमोभिनिघ्नाय रुचये लोकसाक्षिणे॥
And salutation to him that boast of the splendour of burning gold, to the destroyer of all mental obscurity, and to the maker of the universe. Salutation to the remover of darkness; to see illuminator of the Soul; to the allbeholding none of all the words.

नाशयत्येष वै भूतं तमेव सृजति प्रभुः। पायत्येष तपत्येष वर्षत्येष नभस्तिभिः॥
The lord creat everything an verily destroyet it. And with his rays he suck up, and destroy and create (everything)

एष सुप्तेषु जागर्ति भूतेषु परिनिष्ठितः। एष चैवाग्निहोत्रं च फलं चैवाग्निहोत्रिणाम्॥
When all are asleep, this one wake, he is resident in the hearts of all creatures. This one is both Agnihotra as well as the fruit reaped by the sacrifices thereof.

देवाश्च क्रतवश्चैव क्रतूनां फलमेव च। यानि कृत्यानि लोकेषु सर्वेषु परमप्रभुः॥
He constitutes the gods and the sacrifices and the fruit also thereof, and he is the lord of all acts that are performed by creatures

एनमापत्सु कृच्छेषु कान्तारेषु भयेषु च। कीर्तयन्पुरुषः कश्चित्रावसीदति राघव ॥
If a person recites this (hymn), he O Raghava, does not come by misfortune, when he is in peril of his life, or is ill, or in a lonely place, or in fear.

पूजयस्वैनमेकाग्रो देवदेवं जगत्पतिम्। एतत्रिगुणितं जप्त्वा युद्धेषु विजयिष्यति॥
Do you with concentration, worship this god of gods, this lord of the universe. By reciting (this hymn) instinct with the three virtues, you will obtain victory in battle.

अस्मिन्क्षणे महाबाहो रावणं त्वं जहिष्यसि। एवमुक्त्वा ततोऽगस्त्यो जगाम स यथागतम्।।२७
This very instant O mighty-armed one, you will conquer Ravana. Having said this, Agastya went whither from he had come.

एतच्छ्रुत्वा महातेजा नष्टशोकोऽभवत्तदा। धारयामास सुप्रीतो राघवः प्रयतात्मवान् ॥ आदित्यं प्रेक्ष्य जप्त्वेदं परं हर्षमवाप्तवान्। त्रिराजम्य शुचिर्भूत्वा धनुरादाय वीर्यवान्॥ रावणं प्रेक्ष्य हृष्टात्मा जयार्थं समुपागमत्। सर्वयत्नेन महता वृतस्तस्य वधेऽभवत्॥
Hearing this that exceedingly energetic one had his grief gone. then, well pleased, Raghava, exerting himself, contemplated (the hymn). And reciting this he beholding the Sun, attained excess of Joy. And sipping water again and again, and becoming purified, that powerful one, taking up his bow, and viewing Rāvana, advanced with a delighted heart, to obtain victory. And he became intent on his death with his dearest energies.

अथ रविरवदन्निरीक्ष्य राम मुदितमनाः परमं प्रहृष्यमाणः। निशिचरपतिसंक्षयं विदित्वा सुरगणमध्यगतो वचस्त्वरेति॥
Then exceedingly delighted, and filled with rejoicing, the Sun, in the midst of the celestial hosts knowing that the destruction of the Sovereign of the night-rangers was at hand, spoke to Rāma “Bestir yourself.
